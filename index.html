Bio

    How this works

    Setlist reads your vibe as sound — tempo, energy, brightness, groove — and finds the songs that live there, checking each one's genre so a loud pop ballad can't sneak into a storm. Song data comes from ReccoBeats; cover photos are public-domain images from Openverse. Everything is cached in your browser and nothing is sent anywhere else.
